Apply for a protection order

Before you begin

  • We will ask questions about your situation — what happened and when — what protection you need. This online interview can take an hour or more to complete.
  • At the end, you will have the necessary forms to take to court. Protection orders may take 4 weeks or longer. We will ask if you need a temporary protection order to protect you while waiting for the court to hear your case.
  • You can apply yourself. You can also ask a friend or lawyer to apply on your behalf. In some situations, the police may apply for you.
