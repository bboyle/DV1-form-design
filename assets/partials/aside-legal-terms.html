Legal terms

Meaning of terms in the application form for a protection order.

Aggrieved
The person who needs protection.
Applicant
The person applying for a protection order – this can be the aggrieved or another person who can apply on the aggrieved’s behalf.
child, relative or associate named on the protection order
A person also covered by the protection order.
Domestic Violence Order
A protection order or a temporary protection order.
Grounds (for a protection order)
Reasons for the application for a protection order
Ouster Order
An order made by a court stating the respondent must move out of a house or dwelling.
Protection Order
An order made by a court imposing conditions on the respondent.
Respondent
The person you apply to be protected from.
Serve
To deliver or give the application or order to the respondent.
Temporary Protection Order
An order made in the period before a court decides whether to make a protection order.
